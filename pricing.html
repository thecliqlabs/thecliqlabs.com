Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CliqLabs and GoHighLevel white-label support.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
No contracts at all. All CliqLabs plans are billed monthly and you can cancel anytime with no questions asked. No lock-ins, no penalties.
How long does setup take?
Less than 10 minutes. You paste one line of code into your GHL white-label settings and the theme builder and support widget appear instantly.
Will my clients know CliqLabs is powering the support?
Never. Everything is 100% white-labeled under your agency brand. Your clients only ever see your logo, your colors, and your agency name.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
What happens if I cancel and resubscribe later?
All your settings and configurations are saved. When you resubscribe, everything is restored automatically, no manual setup needed.
Is CliqLabs a good alternative to HL Pro Tools or Extendly?
Yes. CliqLabs offers 24/7 white-label GoHighLevel support, a GHL theme builder, done-for-you onboarding, premium snapshots, and weekly coaching, all in one plan starting at $197/month. HL Pro Tools and Extendly charge extra for onboarding and have higher starting prices.
Does CliqLabs work with any GoHighLevel account?
Yes. CliqLabs works with any GoHighLevel agency account. It installs via a single script tag in your GHL white-label settings and works across all your sub-accounts immediately.

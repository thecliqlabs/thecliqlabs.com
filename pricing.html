Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
No contracts at all. All plans are billed monthly and you can cancel anytime with no questions asked.
How long does setup take?
Less than 10 minutes. You paste one line of code into your GHL white-label settings and everything is live instantly.
Will my clients know CliqLabs is powering the support?
Never. Everything is 100% white-labeled under your agency brand. Your clients only see your logo and your name.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the next billing cycle.
What happens if I cancel and resubscribe?
All your settings and configurations are saved. When you resubscribe, everything is restored automatically — no manual setup needed.

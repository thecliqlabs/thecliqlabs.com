Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the CliqLabs GHL Theme Builder.

How does the theme builder work?
You paste one script tag into your GoHighLevel white-label settings. A CliqLabs panel appears inside your GHL sidebar. From there you select a theme and click apply — your entire platform transforms instantly across all sub-accounts.
Do my clients need to do anything?
Nothing. The theme applies automatically to your entire GHL platform including all client sub-accounts. They log in and immediately see the new branded look — no action required from them.
Will it break anything in my GHL account?
No. The theme builder applies CSS styling only — it doesn't touch your GHL data, workflows, automations or funnels. You can remove or switch themes at any time with zero risk.
What's the difference between monthly and annual?
Monthly is $147/mo. Annual is $1,497/year — that's the equivalent of $124.75/mo, saving you $267 compared to paying monthly. Both plans include everything. Annual is billed once per year.
Does it work with the latest version of GoHighLevel?
Yes. We update our themes every time GoHighLevel releases a platform update. Your subscription includes all future updates automatically — you never need to do anything.
Can I get more themes or custom colors?
Your subscription includes all current and future themes at no extra cost. For fully custom brand colors or bespoke theme requests, book a call and we'll discuss options.
